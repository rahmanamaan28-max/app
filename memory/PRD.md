# OnTheGo Travels - Product Requirements Document

## Project Overview
**Website Goal:** Turn visitors into serious enquiries through trust, transparency, and clear communication.

**Business Name:** OnTheGo Travels  
**Social Media:** Telegram - OnTheGo Travels | Instagram - @onthegotravels26

## Core Requirements (Static)
1. Multi-page marketing website with calming, trustworthy aesthetic
2. Light blue/white/navy color palette with amber accents
3. Mobile-first, responsive design
4. Google Form integration for enquiries
5. Transparent pricing breakdown
6. Step-by-step booking process

## User Personas
- **Budget-conscious travelers** seeking honest deals
- **Families and couples** planning trips to North-East India, Himalayas
- **Adventure seekers** looking for custom itineraries to Nepal, Bhutan

## What's Been Implemented (January 30, 2025)

### Pages Completed
1. **Home Page**
   - Hero section with dual CTAs
   - Trust signals (4 cards)
   - Popular destinations grid (6 destinations)
   - How it works (5 steps)
   - Why choose us section
   - Customer reviews (3 testimonials)
   - Final CTA section

2. **Destinations Page**
   - Filterable grid (All, Mountains, Nature, International, South India)
   - 9 destinations: Sikkim, Meghalaya, Tawang, North-Bengal, Himachal, Nepal, Bhutan, Kerala, Tamil Nadu
   - Detail modal with best time, duration, ideal for info

3. **Packages Page**
   - 6 sample itineraries with customizable badge
   - Day-wise breakdown in modal
   - What's included/not included
   - Price ranges

4. **How to Book Page**
   - 6-step vertical timeline
   - Tips for each step
   - Promise section
   - Common FAQs

5. **Pricing & Transparency Page**
   - Sample receipt-style cost breakdown
   - Price factors explained
   - Inclusions/exclusions
   - Payment policy
   - Cancellation policy

6. **About Page**
   - Company story
   - Core values (4 cards)
   - Specializations
   - Social links

7. **Contact Page**
   - Embedded Google Form
   - Alternative contact methods
   - Response time info
   - Working hours

### Components
- Floating glass navbar with "Plan My Trip" CTA
- Footer with social links and quick navigation

### Design System
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (headings), Manrope (body)
- **Colors:** Navy Trust (#0F172A), Calm Sky (#E0F2FE), Adventure Gold (#F59E0B)
- **Border radius:** Rounded 2xl/3xl
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle shadows

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React 19, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn/UI
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Backend:** FastAPI (minimal - static site)
- **Database:** MongoDB (not actively used for this marketing site)
